The Story of Lady Jane

Launched in 2020, Lady Jane was born from a rebellious and cheeky spirit. With colors and finishes inspired by our favorite artists and masterpieces, our goal is to put the art (history) back into nail art. We promise to provide polishes that stun without compromising color, quality, or wit.

FAQ

Does Lady Jane test on animals?

We do not test our products on animals, and instead use recognized alternatives along with existing safety data. All Lady Jane products are reviewed and approved by our incredible team of human safety experts.

Do Lady Jane nail polishes contain formaldehyde, DBP, or toluene?

Our nail polishes do not contain any of these dangerous chemicals. Before we place a product on the market, we assess it thoroughly to ensure it is safe for human use and the environment.

Can I make a suggestion for a product?

We love to hear from you! The best way to reach out and let us know what your favorite artworks are and what colors you want to see next from us is to get in touch on twitter and instagram.
